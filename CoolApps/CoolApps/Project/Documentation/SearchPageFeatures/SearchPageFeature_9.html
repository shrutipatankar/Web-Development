When clicked on "I am hungry" tab, a list of restaurants/cafes/bars/bakeries that appear in the table. With respect to these places, images are shown in a frame that appears at the top left corner beside the map. The images flip with a delay of 3 seconds. The slide show happens only once.
The same feature is also implemented while pulling the data for places Worth visiting
