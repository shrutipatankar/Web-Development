Common Krait



How will you identify me?

  • I have ranged black cells on my back. They are hexagonal in shape
  • There are milky white bands all over my body!
  • I am either black, brown , grey or have a purple tinge
  • I have a prominent milky band near my tail. My tail is sharp
  • I have a triangular head.
  • I am around 90 -120 cm in length
  • I am yellowish white in color on my underside
