HTML5 Audio Experiment

About the experiment

This experiment is to view the html5 audio tag. In order to use this tag, I had to avoid using .wma formats of audio. It supports .mp3,mp4 and ogg formats. I have created a form, which has certain fields. If the field labels are not readable by a Spanish speaking person, he can click on the audio button next to the field for the instructions to enter data in the field. I have recorded instructions in Spanish langauge and uploaded the audio clips beside the respective fields


Code snippet
<audio controls class="myaudio">
<source src="nombre.mp3" type="audio/mp3">
</audio>
